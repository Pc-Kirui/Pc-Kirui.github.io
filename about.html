About Pc

I was born in Tenwek, Bomet county. I was educated in Bomet county for primary and secondary education, and later joined the military. In the military I was trained to be an electrical technician at the Defence Forces Technical College. After graduating from DEFTEC I practiced for one year where I gained valuable skills and knowledge in electrical systems, maintenance and troubleshooting. I later enrolled for a Bsc Degree in Electrical and Electronics Engineering at the University of Nairobi where I anticipate to graduate in September 2023.
I'am excited about the possibilities that lie ahead in my career and aspire to contribute my skills and expertise to make a positive impact in the field of electrical/telecommunication engineering. I hope to work on innovative projects, to continue learning, gain hands on experience and to grow both personally and professionally.
When not working I spend time with my family, being entertained by my daughter, playing taekwondo, swimming or learning new concepts in the field of electrical engineeering.
